About The Show

“Opera the Great” combines music and plots from classic operas in an interactive, fun show for elementary aged children.

The show begins with a brief history of opera exploring its origins and how the performers sing with such loud and talented voices. “Opera the Great” is a tale of two young lovers hopelessly in love seeking the approval of the Soprano’s father to get married. While the Villain attempts to interfere with the happily ever after of the young couple, a Diva sings classical operatic melodies while the Buffo narrates the scenes.

Utilizing a brilliant chest of hats, mustaches and other props, five singers and a talented orchestra of one tell the story. Most importantly student audience members play a crucial and star role as the Bride’s Father and Villain’s Assistant.

Will it be a tragedy or a comedy? The kids decide! The audience members choose whether the young lovers will live in bliss or misery.  After the performance the students are ecstatic and eager for the chance to ask the performers questions.

“Opera the Great” is a unique way to bring an educational, amusing and fun program to your school!
