Welcome to Opera Quest Northwest

Opera Quest Northwest presents an interactive program for students in the Vancouver community. Guided by the simple mission to educate, enrich and instill a love of music in the children of our region, the exciting live program “Opera the Great” explains the history of opera. The story of the show incorporates comedy, tragedy, romance and of course a villain! Students love the show and even get the opportunity to act as featured characters.  At the same time, parents and teachers love the educational component for their students.

Offered at little or no cost to the school, the team of five young professional singers and a pianist have delighted audiences for over three years in the greater Vancouver region. Opera Quest Northwest receives passionate reviews from the audiences and educators with one teacher from Orchards Elementary stating. “It really awakened my students’ appreciation for classical music performance.  Besides this, it’s funny, witty and engaging.  My students talked about it for weeks afterward, and even acted out their favorite parts.”
